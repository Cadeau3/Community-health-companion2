Caregiver Corner

You're doing important work. Small habits protect your well-being — rest, water, and asking for help when you need it.

Quick tips

  • Take short breaks (5–10 min) every few hours.
  • Eat small, regular meals and keep a water bottle near you.
  • Share tasks with family or neighbors when possible.
  • Talk with a teacher or counselor if schoolwork piles up.
